# Global Sports Footwear Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global sports footwear sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, brand performance, customer purchasing behavior, sales channels, and category-wise sales distribution.

The report transforms raw sales data into actionable business intelligence using dynamic visualizations and interactive filtering features in Power BI.

---

# Objectives

The primary goals of this project are:

* Analyze global sports footwear sales trends
* Evaluate revenue performance across brands and categories
* Study customer purchasing behavior and income levels
* Compare sales channels and payment methods
* Monitor yearly sales growth
* Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making

---

# Dataset Overview

The dataset used in this dashboard contains global sports footwear sales records.

## Key Attributes Included

* Order Date
* Brand
* Category
* Country
* Revenue (USD)
* Units Sold
* Final Price (USD)
* Customer Income Level
* Sales Channel
* Payment Method

---

# Dashboard Features

## KPI Cards

The dashboard includes important business KPIs such as:

### Total Revenue

Displays the total revenue generated from sports footwear sales.

### Total Units Sold

Shows the total quantity of products sold.

### Average Final Price

Displays the average final selling price in USD.

---

# Visualizations & Analysis

## 1. Revenue Trend Analysis (Line Chart)

### Purpose

Analyzes yearly revenue trends over time.

### Insights

* Identifies business growth patterns
* Tracks yearly sales performance
* Helps forecast future trends

---

## 2. Customer Income Level vs Revenue (Area Chart)

### Purpose

Analyzes average revenue generated across different customer income levels.

### Business Value

* Understand customer purchasing power
* Identify target customer segments
* Improve marketing strategies

---

## 3. Brand-wise Revenue Analysis (Column Chart)

### Purpose

Compares total revenue generated by different footwear brands.

### Benefits

* Detect top-performing brands
* Compare brand market performance
* Support inventory and marketing decisions

---

## 4. Sales Channel & Payment Method Analysis (Funnel Chart)

### Purpose

Analyzes units sold across different sales channels and payment methods.

### Insights

* Compare online and offline sales channels
* Study customer payment preferences
* Improve sales operations

---

## 5. Sales Channel Revenue Breakdown (Waterfall Chart)

### Purpose

Shows revenue contribution by sales channels and brands.

### Business Benefits

* Evaluate revenue impact of each sales channel
* Identify high-performing brand-channel combinations
* Support sales optimization strategies

---

## 6. Category-wise Revenue Distribution (Donut Chart)

### Purpose

Displays revenue distribution across footwear categories.

### Insights

* Compare category contribution to total revenue
* Identify popular product categories
* Support category-level business planning
